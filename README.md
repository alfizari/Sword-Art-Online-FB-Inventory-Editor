# Sword-Art-Online-FB-Inventory-Editor

An inventory editor for **Sword Art Online: Fatal Bullet**.  

⚠️ **Warning:** Please **backup your save file** before using this tool. There is a high chance it could corrupt your save.  

---

## Features

1. **Edit Weapon Chips** – Modify your equipped weapon chips.  
2. **Modify Item Quantities** – Change the amount of any item in your inventory.  
3. **Replace Items** – Swap items with other items in your inventory.  

---

## Usage

1. Open the editor.  
2. Load your save file.  
3. Select the item, weapon, or chip you want to modify.  
4. Apply your changes and save.
